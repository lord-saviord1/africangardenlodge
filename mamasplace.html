Mama's Place

Boutique Accommodation with African Soul

Where Comfort Meets Culture

Mama's Place is our boutique hotel – a labor of love created by Mama, a talented graphic design artist with a passion for African art. Each room is thoughtfully decorated with carefully curated artwork, textiles, and sculptures from across the continent.

This isn't just accommodation; it's an immersive cultural experience. From the moment you step into your room, you're surrounded by the warmth and richness of African heritage, combined with modern amenities and genuine family hospitality.

Meet Mama

The heart and soul of African Garden Lodge, Mama is a gifted graphic design artist whose love for African culture inspired this unique boutique hotel. Every piece of art, every color choice, and every decorative element reflects her vision of creating a space where guests can experience the beauty and diversity of African creativity.

Mama's personal touch extends beyond the décor. She ensures every guest feels welcomed and cared for, offering recommendations, sharing stories about the artwork, and making sure your stay is memorable. When you stay at Mama's Place, you're not just a guest – you're family.
