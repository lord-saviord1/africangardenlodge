About African Garden Lodge

Located in Omole Phase 1, Ikeja, African Garden Lodge is a hidden gem that combines authentic African hospitality with modern comfort. Our family-run boutique hotel features three distinct venues, each offering a unique experience.

Founded by Mama, a talented graphic design artist, our lodge showcases beautiful African art throughout the property. What started as a serene escape has become one of Lagos's most beloved destinations for both locals and travelers.

  • 30 minutes from Murtala Mohammed Airport
  • Located in highbrow Omole Phase 1
  • Extensive African art collection
  • Family-owned and operated
