The Garden Bar

A Serene Poolside Oasis

Experience Tranquility by the Pool

The Garden Bar is our crown jewel – a natural, serene lounge space that overlooks our beautiful swimming pool. Surrounded by lush greenery and authentic African décor, this is the perfect spot to unwind after a long day in Lagos.

Whether you're enjoying our signature grilled prawns, fresh catfish pepper soup, or simply sipping a cold drink while listening to smooth jazz, the Garden Bar offers an escape from the hustle and bustle of the city.
